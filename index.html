Welcome to the game!

The artwork of this simulation is inspired by the boardgame Splendor©, in which the players transform into Renaissance merchants racing to grab gems to gain the favor of the nobility. Let's begin this epic journey and find some jewels on our way!



Instructions

As in the classic Bejeweled game, in each turn you can click a jewel to grab it and take it with you, together with its equal neighboring jewels (upp, down, left, or right).

For each space left by the eliminated jewels, the upper ones will fall down to replace them. If you collect 4 jewels in a turn, you gain an extra turn! You can hover the collection of jewels to identify the groups that you can take for each possible selection. Good luck!
